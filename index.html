About the Artist

My name is Iulia and I am a Romanian artist working mostly digitally creating various illustrations and artworks. But sometimes I explore new mediums and try out new things. I have a high interest in anything magical or fictional themed, therefore my art is strongly inspired from different fantasy universes.
